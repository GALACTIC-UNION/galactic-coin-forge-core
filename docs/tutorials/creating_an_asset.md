# Creating an Asset

## Introduction

In this tutorial, you will learn how to create a new digital asset on the Galactic Coin Forge platform.

## Step 1: Access the Assets Section

1. Log in to your account.
2. Click on the "Assets" tab in the navigation menu.

## Step 2: Add a New Asset

1. Click on the "Add Asset" button.
2. Fill in the asset details:
   - **Asset Name**: Enter a name for your asset.
   - **Description**: Provide a brief description of the asset.
   - **Initial Quantity**: Specify the initial quantity of the asset.
3. Click on the "Create Asset" button.

## Step 3: Confirm Creation

Once the asset is created, you will see a confirmation message. Your new asset will now appear in the assets list.

## Conclusion

You have successfully created a new digital asset! Continue to the next tutorial to learn how to send assets to other users.
